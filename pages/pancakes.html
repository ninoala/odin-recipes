Banana Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a universal stamp in many breakfasts of families around the world. Adding bananas with your pancakes aims to create a rich flavour and an infusion of vitamines and fiber. This is exactly what you need to start your day in the best way possible.

Check out the recipe below for banana pancakes made from scratch and enjoy your healthy morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Steps

  1. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder and salt. In a separate bowl, mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil and bananas.
  2. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture; batter will be slightly lumpy.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides; serve hot.
