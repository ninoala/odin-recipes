Walnut Salad

Description

Brought to us by emergence of the fall, this apple walnut salad can be thrown together super fast. Itâ€™s mixed with an incredible balsamic vinaigrette and can be served with practically any kind of dinner dish!

This walnut salad is the perfect combo of tastes, with sweet and sour notes complimenting each other as they mix in your mouth. Aaaand.. did I tell you? It is also very simple mix of ingridients! So it can easily become your go to salad for any kind of night, e.g. a potluck party.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups chopped red leaf lettuce, about 1 large head
  • 1 medium apple, cored and thinly sliced (I used Gala apple)
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onions
  • 3/4 cup walnut halves, toasted + roughly chopped
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese

For the Vinaigrette

  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp. honey or maple syrup
  • Kosher salt and fresh ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the vinaigrette: in a small bowl, combine the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, mustard, and honey.
  2. Whisk until blended. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. Assemble the salad: in a large bowl combine the lettuce, apples, red onions, walnuts, and feta.
  4. Drizzle with desired amount of vinaigrette and serve immediately.
